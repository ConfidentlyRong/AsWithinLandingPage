Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters.

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Overview

As Within is a wellness app designed to support meditation, breathwork, mindfulness, reminders, and self-guided inner practice. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, such as account information, app preferences, reminder settings, and support messages. We may also collect basic app usage information to help us understand how the app is being used and how we can improve it.

Health and Wellness Information

As Within may allow you to create or save meditation, breathwork, reminder, or wellness-related preferences. This information is used to personalize your experience within the app.

As Within is not a medical service and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

How We Use Information

We use information to operate the app, personalize your experience, provide reminders and app features, respond to support requests, improve performance, and maintain the safety and reliability of our services.

Notifications

If you choose to enable notifications, As Within may send reminders related to meditation, breathwork, daily practice, or other app features. You can manage or disable notifications through your device settings at any time.

Purchases and Subscriptions

If you purchase a subscription, payment and subscription management are handled through Apple and/or our subscription service providers. We do not store your full payment card information.

Third-Party Services

As Within may use trusted third-party services for features such as authentication, subscriptions, analytics, crash reporting, cloud storage, or app functionality. These services may process information according to their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only when needed to operate the app, comply with legal obligations, protect our rights, or support services you choose to use.

Data Retention

We keep information only as long as reasonably necessary to provide the app, support your account, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Your Choices

You may be able to update, delete, or manage certain information directly in the app. You can also disable notifications through your device settings and manage subscriptions through your Apple account.

Children’s Privacy

As Within is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact us through the support page.
